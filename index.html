How I Work

A thoughtful process from data to decision

Understand

I start by listening to your business goals and the decisions you need to make — before touching any data.

Collect & Clean

Raw data gets structured, validated, and transformed into a reliable foundation ready for analysis.

Analyse

I uncover patterns, trends, and outliers that answer your key questions using SQL, Python, or spreadsheet tools.

Visualise

Findings are translated into polished dashboards or presentations — with careful attention to color harmony and visual clarity.

Deliver

You receive a clean, documented deliverable with a walkthrough, ready to share with your team or clients.
